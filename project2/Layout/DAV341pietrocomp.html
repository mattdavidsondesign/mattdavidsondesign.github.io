Pietro’s Pizza Near Me

Pietro’s Pizza brings fond memories to families in Portland. Everybody can remember their favorite restaurants when they were a kid, and for many people in Milwaukie, Salem, Hood River, and Beaverton, it’s the delicious thin crust pizzas from Pietro’s.

Pietro’s is a great restaurant for hosting birthday parties and celebrations for sports teams, but adults love our pizza as well. We use fresh cheese and toppings with our famous crust and sauce that have pleased the whole family since we opened our doors in 1957. Many people say it’s the best pizza in Portland!
