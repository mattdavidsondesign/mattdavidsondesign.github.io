Pietro’s Birthday Party Packages

Party Packages (12 and under) are $7.50 per child and include a Kid’s One-Topping Pizza, soft drink, 4 Game Tokens, Ice Cream Cone and Themed Table Decorations. Themes include:

  • Star Wars
  • Spider-Man
  • Frozen
  • Pirates (may indicate # of boys & girls)
  • Minions
  • Disney Princess
  • Happy Birthday (Generic)

Mini Golf can be added to any birthday package for only $4/child (Salem & Beaverton locations only).

Laser Tag is only an additional $5/child with purchase of a birthday party package (Salem & Beaverton locations only).

You can also add great pirate fun with “the Swashbuckler” for only $3 (Salem & Beaverton locations only) – it includes a pirate sword, eye patch and hat.
