A Different Shade Of Blue

A DIFFERENT
SHADE OF
BLUE

2019

A Different Shade of Blue is the second studio album by American hardcore punk band Knocked Loose. It was released through Pure Noise Records on 23 August 2019 and produced by Will Putney.

Ahead of the album, a short EP was released containing “Mistakes Like Fractures” alongside a rerecording of “All My Friends” and a cover of “Slings and Arrows” by The Warriors.
